title: "Annual Sports Day Celebration 2025"
date: 2025-01-15

---

Veer Patta Public School celebrated its Annual Sports Day with great enthusiasm. Students from all classes participated in various track and field events, showcasing their athletic talents.

Key highlights:
- 100m, 200m, and relay races
- Shot put and long jump competitions
- March past by all houses
- Award ceremony for outstanding athletes

The event was graced by local dignitaries and parents who cheered for the young champions.
