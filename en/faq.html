Admissions

Our admission process is simple: 1) Visit the school or contact us via WhatsApp/phone, 2) Fill out the admission form, 3) Provide required documents (birth certificate, previous school records, photos), 4) Pay the admission fee. Admissions are open throughout the year based on seat availability.

A child must be at least 3 years old as of April 1st of the admission year to be eligible for nursery class admission.

For nursery to Class 5, no entrance test is required. For Class 6 and above, we conduct a simple assessment to understand the student's current level and provide appropriate support.

Required documents include: Birth certificate, Previous school transfer certificate (TC), Aadhar card copy, Passport-size photographs (4), Parent/Guardian ID proof, and Previous year mark sheets (if applicable).

Yes, mid-year admissions are accepted subject to seat availability. We provide catch-up support to help new students adjust to our curriculum.

Academics

Veer Patta Public School is affiliated with the Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education (RBSE). We follow the RBSE curriculum from nursery to Class 12.

LEAD is an integrated school learning system that brings excellence in curriculum, pedagogy, and technology to classrooms. It helps students build strong conceptual foundations through multimedia lessons, regular assessments, and personalized learning.

The medium of instruction is English for all subjects except Hindi. We ensure that students are comfortable with both English and Hindi languages.

Yes, we provide free remedial classes after school hours for students who need additional support. Our teachers are committed to ensuring every child succeeds.

We offer three streams: Science (PCM/PCB), Commerce, and Arts. Students can choose their stream based on their interests and career goals.

Facilities

Yes, we have well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology laboratories where students conduct practical experiments as per the RBSE curriculum.

We have facilities for cricket, football, volleyball, kho-kho, and athletics. We also have indoor game facilities and a dedicated sports period in the timetable.

Yes, we have a well-stocked library with textbooks, reference books, story books, and magazines. Students can borrow books and use the library during designated hours.

Yes, safety is our top priority. We have CCTV surveillance throughout the campus, security personnel at the gate, and strict entry/exit protocols for students and visitors.

Yes, we have a computer lab where students learn basic computer skills, programming, and digital literacy from Class 1 onwards.

Fees & Payment

Our fee structure is designed to be affordable for families in the Amet region. Fees vary by class and include tuition, books, and basic facilities. For detailed fee information, please contact our office or visit the school.

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for top-performing students and sibling discounts when multiple children from the same family are enrolled. Fee concessions are also available on a case-by-case basis for deserving students.

Yes, we understand the financial constraints of families and offer flexible payment options. Fees can be paid quarterly or monthly as per your convenience.

Transport fees vary based on the distance from the school. We have bus routes covering Amet town and surrounding villages. Contact us for specific route information and fees.

General Information

School timing varies by season: Summer (April-September): 7:30 AM - 1:30 PM. Winter (October-March): 8:30 AM - 2:30 PM. Office hours: Monday-Saturday, 8:00 AM - 2:00 PM.

Yes, we have school buses covering Amet town and nearby villages including Khempur, Parasoli, and others. New routes can be arranged based on demand.

VPS has an active NCC (National Cadet Corps) unit where students learn discipline, leadership, and national service. Students can participate in parades, camps, and earn NCC certificates (A, B, C levels).

We maintain regular communication with parents through WhatsApp updates, SMS notifications, parent-teacher meetings (held quarterly), and progress reports. Parents can also contact teachers directly for concerns.

Yes, we organize various co-curricular activities including annual day, sports day, science exhibitions, cultural programs, drawing competitions, and debates to help students develop their overall personality.
