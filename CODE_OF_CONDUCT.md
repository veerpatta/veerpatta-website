# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our project a respectful and professional experience for everyone involved in the Veer Patta Public School website.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

* Using welcoming and inclusive language
* Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
* Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
* Focusing on what is best for the school community
* Showing empathy towards other community members
* Providing helpful and constructive feedback
* Maintaining professionalism in all interactions

### Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

* The use of inappropriate language or imagery
* Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal attacks
* Public or private harassment
* Publishing others' private information without permission
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
* Deliberately providing false or misleading information
* Spamming or disrupting discussions

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

* GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, discussions)
* Project documentation
* Code commits and reviews
* Any project-related communications

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at veerpatta.school@gmail.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions.

## Guidelines for Contributors

### For Developers and Coding Agents

* Write clean, well-documented code
* Follow the project's coding standards and guidelines
* Test your changes before submitting
* Respect the existing architecture and patterns
* Maintain bilingual parity (English/Hindi)
* Prioritize accessibility and mobile-first design
* Be patient and helpful when reviewing others' work

### For Content Contributors

* Ensure accuracy of information about the school
* Maintain professional tone in all content
* Respect copyright and intellectual property
* Provide both English and Hindi translations
* Use appropriate and respectful images
* Keep content suitable for a school website

### For Non-Technical Users

* Ask questions respectfully
* Provide clear descriptions when reporting issues
* Be patient while waiting for responses
* Follow provided guides and documentation
* Respect maintainers' time and expertise
